About Us

Since the dawn of civilizations, youth have played a significant role in determining the course of society. The adage "the youth of today are the future of tomorrow" is well known to all of us. The youth have the power to change the trajectory of history. They are the motivation behind shaping the country for a brighter future.

To catalyse the vitality of the youth and provide a platform for constructive activism, Youth United for Vision and Action (YUVA) was established in 2016. YUVA is a youth-based, youth-run platform for Academic, Social, and Cultural endeavours.

Objectives of YUVA

  • To support young people's enthusiasm and provide them with a venue for engaging in meaningful activism.
  • To provide a platform for students to organize, perform, and participate in Academic, Cultural, Social, and Sports Activities.
  • To encourage students to participate in learning activities, hone leadership, and organizational skills.
  • To offer a shared forum for conversation and the exchange of ideas among the youth.
  • To redesign the current research environment and foster innovation in methodology and philosophy.

Our Mission

To unite and empower youth to drive societal change through impactful actions and community welfare initiatives.

Our Vision

To foster a vibrant youth network committed to innovation, inclusivity, and sustainable development.

Our Values

Collaboration, compassion, and courage form the foundation of our efforts to create a positive societal impact.
